It's me, Mario!

I'm Mario, the lovable Italian plumber who’s always ready to save the day! I've been jumping, running, and power-upping my way through the Mushroom Kingdom and beyond since 1981. Whether it’s rescuing Princess Peach from the clutches of Bowser, racing through rainbow-colored tracks in my go-kart, or playing a friendly game of tennis with my pals, there’s never a dull moment in my life!
